Authentic Grace 2.0

As another assignment from Cohort 4 of the Moms Can: Code School we were to add few features to the Authentic Grace storefront. We could also change things from the original design to make it more our own. I added a product page that could be accessed by clicking the "Shirts" image under "Our Products" on the homepage.

We were also tasked to create an accessibility report based on the homepage. This brought to my attention many things to update on the homepage to make it more accessible and user friendly. As a person without any disabilities I was not aware of all the things that could be done to make your website usable by those who need screenreaders or other help using the internet.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flexbox, Figma, Git, GitHub, Netlify
