HTML Newsletter



This newsletter is the result of a pair programming project to build an html email from a imaginary company. We chose Union Square Farmers Market in Boston, MA. The business we chose is not imaginary but our newsletter is. My teammate, Aiko Takemura and I communicated through Slack and video conferencing. We chose to pair program through VS Code Live Share and created our repo on GitHub.

This was my first experience in working remotely on a team and coding through pair programming. This taught me ways I could improve upon my communcation skills and how to successfully manage a project when two people live in different states with different schedules.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, Git, GitHub, VS Code Live Share
